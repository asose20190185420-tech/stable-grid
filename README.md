Stable Grids is a spiritual successor to the power-plant tycoon genre, specifically designed to
bridge the gap between casual management games and high-fidelity industrial simulations.
Unlike traditional titles where power generation is abstract, this project focuses on the physics
of the thermal cycle, the instability of the electrical grid, and the logistics of geographical
cooling.
